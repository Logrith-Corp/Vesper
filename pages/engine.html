How it works

From keystroke
to completion.

01

Intent Detection

Vesper's neural layer monitors typing patterns, cursor movement, and tab state to build a real-time model of what you're about to do.

02

Resource Prefetch

Predicted URLs, APIs, and assets are prefetched in the background — fully rendered and ready before you finish typing.

03

Context Assembly

Relevant context from open tabs, bookmarks, and local notes is assembled and ranked by semantic relevance to your current intent.

04

Autonomous Execution

If a task requires multiple steps — research, form completion, data extraction — the Autonomous Layer handles it in the background.

05

Silent Delivery

Results surface exactly when you need them, without interrupting your flow. The engine disappears into the work.
